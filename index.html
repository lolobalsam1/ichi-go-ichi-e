the project

This window has a protocol. People pass it quickly — it is a corridor, a between-place. Nobody lingers. But the light changes here, and something always seems to be playing in someone's ears.

This page is a gesture: a small addition to that protocol. A place to leave a trace.

Protocol

People move through fast. It is transitional — a connector, not a destination. The window frames outside light that nobody stops to look at.

What I wanted to reveal

The invisible layer: what everyone is privately hearing while they pass. Music as inner weather, made briefly visible here.

Why this addition

A QR code costs nothing. A shared journal transforms a corridor into a site of collective feeling — without changing the architecture at all.

Impact

You're reading this. Someone else left something. The window is now a conversation between strangers who never met here at the same time.
