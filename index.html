request a song

Is there a song you think should be on the mp3 player? Approved songs get downloaded, named after their best lyric or the feeling they left, and added to the player at the window.

Songs with lyrics are named after a line. Instrumentals after the feeling they left. No prefixes — just phrases.

Approved requests are downloaded, renamed to your lyric, and added to the mp3 player at the window — so the next person who picks up the headphones plays a song someone else left for them.
